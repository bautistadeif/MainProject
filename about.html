ABOUT US

At DEI's Motorshop, we believe that a diverse and inclusive workplace is the key to driving innovation and excellence in the automotive industry. Our mission is to provide top-notch car repair and maintenance services while fostering a culture of belonging, respect, and empowerment for all our employees and customers.

OUR STORY


Founded by a team of passionate car enthusiasts from diverse backgrounds, DEI's Motorshop is committed to breaking down barriers and stereotypes in the automotive industry. We believe that everyone deserves equal opportunities to succeed, regardless of their race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, or ability.
